LANGUAGE
- Write the values of "answer" and "quick_replies" in the requested language.

INTENT
Set "intent" to exactly one of: "procedure_guidance", "form_guidance", "general", or "out_of_scope".

OUTPUT

Rules:
- Do not add extra fields.
